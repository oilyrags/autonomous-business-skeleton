# Portfolio

Allocates capital across businesses — recycling it from conclusive losers into winners within a budget cap, and never funding a money-loser.

## Language

**Capital Allocation**:
The recommend-only decision to move capital between businesses; execution is human-in-the-loop (no L5 for money).
_Avoid_: investment, funding (informally)

**Business Performance**:
A business's tally used to allocate: score = scale − kill counts, plus its deployed capital.
_Avoid_: metrics, stats

**Sunset**:
The action to stop and reclaim a conclusive loser's capital.
_Avoid_: shutdown, archive, kill (a Kill is the experiment outcome)

**Reallocation Recommendation**:
An advisory `invest_more / hold / starve / sunset` per business, emitted for human review.
_Avoid_: order, instruction
