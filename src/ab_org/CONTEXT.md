# Org

The hierarchical model of who may decide what: agent charters, authority levels, and escalation up the reporting chain — including to a human.

## Language

**Charter**:
An agent's role, authority level, department, and who it reports to; the unit of the org graph.
_Avoid_: role, job, profile

**Authority Level**:
The autonomy (0–5) a process or agent is granted, per the autonomy authority matrix; L5 (money/high-risk) is restricted.
_Avoid_: permission tier, access level

**Escalation**:
Routing a decision that needs more authority than the initiator holds up the `reports_to` chain — to a human if no agent qualifies.
_Avoid_: handoff, approval request
