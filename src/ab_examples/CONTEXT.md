# Examples (not a bounded context)

Worked examples that compose the real bounded contexts end to end — no domain model of their own.
`inboxiq.py` is the canonical one: a fictional B2B SaaS provisioned, marketed, sold, monetized,
measured, capital-allocated, and monitored entirely on stub adapters and the in-memory ledger.
