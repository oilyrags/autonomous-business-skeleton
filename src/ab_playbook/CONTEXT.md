# Living Playbooks

Distils the strategies of winning businesses into a versioned, reusable Blueprint template — aggregate-only, so learning transfers without leaking any one business.

## Language

**Playbook**:
A versioned template distilled from winners: median economics + the modules a majority used; carries no business_id.
_Avoid_: template, strategy (informally)

**Pattern Extraction**:
Deriving the recurring, aggregate characteristics of winning businesses (frequency-ranked), never per-business detail.
_Avoid_: mining, analysis
