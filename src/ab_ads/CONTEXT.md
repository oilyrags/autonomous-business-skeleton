# Ads

Paid acquisition: spend is placed through an ad-platform port and booked to the ledger, and attributed conversions close the loop so CAC is real.

## Language

**Campaign**:
A business-scoped ad spend on a channel; booked as an outbound (maker-checker) ledger payment to `external:ad:<channel>`.
_Avoid_: ad, boost (a boost is amplification of an existing post), promotion

**Attribution**:
Linking ad spend to the conversions it produced, so CAC = spend / conversions on real numbers.
_Avoid_: tracking, credit
